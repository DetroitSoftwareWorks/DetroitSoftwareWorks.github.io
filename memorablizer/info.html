Make Secure Passwords Memorable

The Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF) recommends the use of dice to generate passwords that are both secure and easy to remember. This method is time-consuming, but if you need the level of security it provides, it may be your best option.

If youâ€™re looking for a more convenient approach, this app is for you.

Memorablizer converts random, secure passwords generated by password managers (from an app store or your web browser) to passphrases that are just as random but easier to remember—more memorable.

Note: At the time of publication, the EFF recommended using six words in your passphrase for sensitive applications like your disk encryption and your password manager.
